Our Journey

The SMIT Heritage

"Mula sa pangarap na makapagbigay ng de-kalidad na edukasyon sa Mindanao, ang **Southern Mindanao Institute of Technology Inc.** ay patuloy na naglilingkod at humuhubog ng mga lider na may integridad at kakayahan sa teknolohiya."

VISION

SOUTHERN MINDANAO INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, INC. IS A LEARNER-CENTERED INSTITUTE COMMITED TO QUALITY EDUCATION.

MISSION

SOUTHERN MINDANAO INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY, INC. IS A NON-SECTARIAN SCHOOL COMMITS TO PROVIDE THE COMMUNITY WITH COMPITENT, ETHICAL AND RESEARCH ORIENTED GRADUATES THROUGH: A. EFFECTIVE AND EFFIECENT FACULTY AND STAFF B. ADEQUATE AND APPROPRIATES INSTRUCTIONAL FACILITIES C. INNOVATIVE AND RESPONSIVE TEACHING STRATIGIES
